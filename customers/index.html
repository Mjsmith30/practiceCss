Keith Kilgore

Founder of HTEW fitness

Peace and blessings to each person. My name is Keith Kilgore Jr and I would love to coach you on being the best version of you. Becoming the best isn’t always about medals, trophies or even financial gain, but rather an exploration into your deeper subconscious through human movement. Im a current certified personal trainer through NASM, and former football player. Im also currently an avid participant in strength and conditioning to be a competitive runner, Marathon Runner, and ultramarathon runner. I ran the Ventura Marathon with a time of 3:05:09 and I’ve run UltraMarathons up to 30 miles with more competitive races in the coming years. I would love to help you reach not just your goals, but to make better ones along the way. Every trainer has what works for them, but it’s most important what works for you! Programming for you isn’t part of a template, it’s personally made for you! Let me get to know you because you already possess all that you need, I’m just here to help guide you!

The son of a military vet and a life long two job mother I have traveled a lot over my life time but I call Sunny Southern California home. I was raised in Inglewood while attending Santa Monica High School. I currently live in Camarillo but love training clients all over the area! I specialize in making athletic movements a part of your daily routine! If your ready to take on the challenge of getting to know yourself better than you already do...come be a part of HTEW Fitness. Because fitness isn’t just a gym, it’s HERE, THERE, and EVERYWHERE. Look forward to hearing from you
